My name is Ann-Sofie (she/her) and I am a PostDoc at Physics of Ice, Climate and Earth at the Niels Bohr Institute, University of Copenhagen. I am passionate about bringing the remote sensing and modelling communities together, with the aim of better understanding the Earth at present, past, and in the future.

In my PhD I developed BURGEE: Basal melt rates Using REMA and Google Earth Engine. With BURGEE I am able to assess ice shelf basal melting at a high spatial resolution, which has increased our understanding of basal channels and their potential impact on ice shelf integrity.
